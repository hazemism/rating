My Taste in Movies

My taste in movies leans toward science-based stories that make me think deeply. I like films where the science isn’t just decoration, but the core idea behind the plot. Movies that give me the feeling there’s something huge and hidden beneath the surface.

I enjoy films like Interstellar and Dune, because they build whole worlds on real scientific or philosophical foundations. I also connect with movies like Arrival, Ex Machina, Blade Runner 2049, Annihilation, Solaris, and Tenet—anything that mixes science with deep meaning and leaves me thinking long after it ends.
